Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Claw.Toys ("we", "our", "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit and use our website at claw.toys (the "Service"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide and improve our Service:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, browser type, device type, operating system, screen resolution, and referring URL.
  • Game Data: Game scores, preferences, and session statistics stored locally in your browser via localStorage.
  • Analytics Data: Anonymous usage patterns collected through Google Analytics 4 and Microsoft Clarity to understand how visitors interact with our site.
  • Referral Data: If you arrive via a referral link, we may store a referral identifier in your browser.

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Service and game experience
  • To analyze usage patterns and optimize site performance
  • To track aggregate statistics for game leaderboards
  • To prevent abuse and ensure fair gameplay

4. Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to save your game progress, preferences, and session data. Third-party analytics services (Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity) may set cookies to collect anonymous usage data. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work as expected.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Google Analytics 4: Collects anonymous usage statistics. Google Privacy Policy
  • Microsoft Clarity: Records anonymous session replays and heatmaps. Microsoft Privacy Statement
  • GitHub Pages: Hosts this website. GitHub Privacy Statement

6. Data Retention

Game data is stored locally on your device and persists until you clear your browser data. Analytics data is retained according to the respective third-party service policies (Google Analytics retains data for 14 months by default).

7. Children's Privacy

Our Service is a free entertainment platform. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. All game data is stored locally on the user's device. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect information. The site is served over HTTPS. Since game data is stored locally in your browser, you retain full control over it and can delete it at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear all locally stored game data through your browser settings
  • Opt out of Google Analytics using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Disable cookies and tracking through your browser settings
  • Request information about data we may hold related to your visit

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, you can reach us through our GitHub organization at github.com/Spunkeroo.
